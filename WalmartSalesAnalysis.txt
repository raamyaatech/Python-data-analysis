About the data
This data is obtained from Kaggle Walmart sales forecasting competition. It contains sales transactions of three different branches of Walmart

Product analysis
Perform the analysis on the dataset to understand the best performing products, and identify the areas for improvement in other products

Sales analysis is used to understand the sales trends of the products.
This analysis helps to understand the different sales strategy applied in the business and indentify the necessary changes
to improve the sales.

Customer analysis is used to understand the customer segments,purchase trends and profitability of customer segments.


Walmart Sales data analyzing is used to understand the sales trends, buying behaviour of customers and most buying products.
